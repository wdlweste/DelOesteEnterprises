The official render of Mandurriao Global City

Mandurriao Global City is poised to become the largest and most modern lifestyle and business district in the Philippines. Spanning an impressive 250 hectares across the vibrant Iloilo City districts of Mandurriao and Molo, this ambitious development is elegantly divided by the picturesque Iloilo River. Developed by Mandurriao Developments Corp., a subsidiary of Del Oeste Enterprises, Inc., MGC is envisioned as a seamless fusion of cutting-edge infrastructure and lush, green environmental landscapes. The project will incorporate advanced technologies like artificial intelligence into every facet of its design, from smart, efficient roads to innovative, intelligent buildings. This forward-thinking approach will not only revolutionize urban living but also provide unparalleled convenience and efficiency, establishing Mandurriao Global City as a truly prodigious modern lifestyle hub.

Mandurriao Developments Corp. is a real estate developer that primarily manages and operates Mandurriao Global City, which is set to be the largest and most advanced lifestyle and business district in the Philippines. Spanning an impressive 250 hectares across the vibrant districts of Mandurriao and Molo in Iloilo City, this ambitious development is beautifully divided by the scenic Iloilo River. MGC aims to blend state-of-the-art infrastructure with lush, green landscapes.

The project will integrate cutting-edge technologies, including artificial intelligence, into its design, featuring smart roads and intelligent buildings. This forward-looking vision will not only transform urban living but also enhance convenience and efficiency, positioning Mandurriao Global City as a premier modern lifestyle destination.

Additionally, the company plans to expand into various areas across Western Visayas and the rest of the Philippines. Among the developments in the pipeline are:

  • Costa de Sanao in Guimaras, a 1,100-hectare residential and urban resort development.

  • La Punta Nuevo in Iloilo City, a 50-hectare vertical residential and commercial development situated on a reclaimed island south of Iloilo City Proper.

  • Roxas Business Park, a 90-hectare mixed-use development in Roxas City, Capiz.

  • Distrito de Pasi, a 320-hectare residential and commercial development in Passi City, Iloilo province.

  • Kalibo International District, a 65-hectare mixed-use development in Kalibo, Aklan.

  • San Jose de Distrito Comercial, a 70-hectare mixed-use development in San Jose de Buenavista, envisioned to be the new business center of Antique.

Real estate developments are also in the pipeline in Koronadal and Tacurong in Mindanao.

Del Oeste Technologies, Inc. is a cutting-edge tech company specializing in city automation solutions that enhance urban living through smart technology. The company focuses on creating integrated systems that streamline city operations, improve public safety, and increase overall efficiency. Their innovations include smart traffic management, energy-efficient infrastructure, and advanced communication networks, all designed to support sustainable urban development.

Del Oeste Technologies is also responsible for the advanced technologies implemented in Mandurriao Global City. By integrating artificial intelligence and other state-of-the-art solutions into the city’s design, the company aims to create a seamless urban experience. This includes features such as intelligent buildings, smart transportation systems, and real-time data analytics to monitor and optimize city functions. Through its efforts, Del Oeste Technologies is helping to establish Mandurriao Global City as a model for future urban developments in the Philippines and beyond.

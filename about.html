Del Oeste Enterprises, Inc. is a prominent conglomerate that encompasses a diverse range of companies within the real estate and technology sectors. Founded in 2024 by visionary entrepreneur Winlord del Oeste, the company is headquartered in the innovative Mandurriao Global City in Iloilo City, Philippines. This strategic location not only highlights the company’s commitment to modern urban development but also serves as a hub for its various business ventures.

As one of the largest holding companies in Western Visayas, Del Oeste Enterprises is dedicated to driving economic growth and technological advancement in the region. The conglomerate’s real estate arm focuses on developing sustainable and integrated communities, while its technology division is committed to pioneering solutions that enhance urban living through smart automation and connectivity. Del Oeste Enterprises prioritizes innovation and sustainability, striving to create environments that improve the quality of life for residents and businesses alike. With a strong emphasis on corporate social responsibility, the company engages in initiatives that support community development, environmental conservation, and educational programs.

Under Winlord del Oeste’s leadership, the conglomerate is poised for continued growth, with plans for expansion both within the Philippines and internationally. By leveraging its expertise in real estate and technology, Del Oeste Enterprises aims to redefine modern living and set new standards for excellence in both industries.
WINLORD DEL OESTE
Founder, Chief Executive Officer (CEO), Chairman, and President of Del Oeste Enterprises, Inc.
Winlord Christian Gonzales del Oeste was born and raised in Iloilo City, a major Philippine urban area that has long been envisioned as a smart city by its leaders. From a young age, Del Oeste cultivated a passion for technology, architecture, and urban development. His interest in construction led him to pursue a degree in civil engineering at the University of San Agustin, where he honed his skills and deepened his understanding of innovative design and infrastructure.

With a vision to transform his home district of Mandurriao and elevate Iloilo City as a whole, Del Oeste founded Del Oeste Enterprises, Inc. This company was established to turn his aspirations into reality, focusing on creating sustainable and modern developments that enhance urban living. Recognizing the interconnectedness of real estate and technology in building smart cities, he later founded Mandurriao Developments Corp. and Del Oeste Technologies, Inc. These collaborative firms were designed to work synergistically, fostering innovation and excellence in the creation of smart urban environments in the Philippines.

Del Oeste is dedicated to leveraging cutting-edge technology to improve city infrastructure, enhance quality of life, and promote sustainability. Under his leadership, the companies aim to set new benchmarks in urban development, blending modern architecture with smart technologies to create integrated, efficient communities. His commitment to community development and corporate social responsibility reflects his desire to give back to the city that shaped him, ensuring that Iloilo City thrives as a model for future smart cities in the region and beyond.
